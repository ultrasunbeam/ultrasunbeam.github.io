PROJECTS


Museum Identity Brand Project

Fall 2020

As part of the Visual Communication Design II course, students are tasked to develop a brand identity system for a for a fictional museum. The final products include a logo design, a stationary system, a visual identity system, and a visual guidelines book.

UX/UI Productivity App Team Project

Fall 2020

As part of the 5D Workshop course, students are put together into teams and are tasked to design an app that can be realistically released on the app store. Designers are required to conduct research, develop wire flows and user flows, and develop a hi-fi prototype on Adobe XD. In a team, students learn how to cooperate and work from different locations. My role was to develop the prototype for one of the app's features.
